I am **Sabuj C. Bhowmick**, an MSc Data Analytics student at the University of Turku, Finland, with an interdisciplinary background in statistics, mathematical sciences, public health, IoT engineering, and computational research.

My work focuses on building reproducible, interpretable, and methodologically rigorous data-driven solutions across machine learning, applied statistics, bioinformatics, computational biology, and data-intensive systems.

I am particularly interested in **multiomics integration, microbiome data science, time-series forecasting, machine learning validation, statistical modeling, and research-oriented software development**.

My current technical direction combines **Python, R, SQL, Bioconductor, machine learning pipelines, statistical modeling, Git/GitHub workflows, and reproducible scientific reporting**.
